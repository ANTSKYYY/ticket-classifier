## Phase 1: Data Preparation (Milestone S4 - Arthur)

[Detailed work](https://github.com/ANTSKYYY/deeplearning/blob/main/docs/personnalwork/Arthur.md)

Arthur is in charge of data acquisition, cleaning, and preparation (S4). The old dataset (339 samples) has been replaced by a much more robust database to enable effective deep learning.

* **The Dataset:** It now contains approximately 4,000 customer support ticket samples [Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tobiasbueck/multilingual-customer-support-tickets).
* **Classes and Distribution:** The dataset is divided into three categories: *Support*, *Feature Request*, and *Billing and Payments*.
* **Balancing (Undersampling):** To prevent a massive bias, the majority class (*Support*) is capped at a maximum of 2,000 samples before being mixed with the other classes.
* **Advanced Cleaning:** Application of a Regex pipeline (`clean_text_bert`) to standardize greetings, signatures, versions, and specific software names (e.g., replacement with generic tokens like `SOFTWARE_PRODUCT`).

## Phase 3: Advanced Deep Learning Model (Milestone S11 - Arslan)

[Detailed work](https://github.com/ANTSKYYY/deeplearning/blob/main/docs/personnalwork/Arslan.md)

Arslan has completely redesigned the Deep Learning approach with a DistilBERT pipeline integrating **Adversarial Training** and **Curriculum Learning** (S11).

* **Architecture & Curriculum Learning:** The fine-tuning of `distilbert-base-uncased` is now performed in two progressive stages:
* **Phase 1 (Normal / Easy Data):** The model learns to build solid semantic representations on the clean dataset (LR = 1.5e-5, 2 epochs).
* **Phase 2 (Hard / Adversarial Data):** Injection of adversarial examples (oversampled to reach a 1:3 ratio, or 25% of the augmented dataset). The learning rate is drastically reduced (LR = 5e-6) to adjust ambiguous decision boundaries without causing the catastrophic forgetting of Phase 1 learnings.


* **Imbalance Management (WeightedTrainer):** Creation of a custom training class injecting dynamic class weights (`class_weights`) directly into the Cross-Entropy loss function.

## Phase 4: Evaluation and Error Analysis (Milestone S14 - Evan)

## Phase 5: Risk Management and Final Defense (Milestone S15 - Enzo)

[Detailed work](https://github.com/ANTSKYYY/deeplearning/blob/main/docs/personnalwork/Enzo.md)

Enzo is responsible for preparing the final defense and finalizing the repository (S15), incorporating new arguments related to Arslan and Arthur's modifications.

* **Data Risk Mitigation:** The initial problem (a dataset that was too small with 339 samples) was resolved by acquiring the new 4,000-line dataset.
* **Model Fragility Mitigation:** The typical vulnerability of NLP models to attacks or noise is directly countered by **Phase 2 (Adversarial Training)** of Arslan's pipeline.
* **Imbalance and Forgetting Mitigation:** The risk of only predicting the majority class is managed mathematically by weight injection (`WeightedTrainer`). The risk of *catastrophic forgetting* during phase 2 is managed by reducing the *learning rate*.
